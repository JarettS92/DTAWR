What is this?

The Consultant Portal is a collection of custom tools, reports, and dashboards developed by consultants in the field.

Why was this developed?

This portal was created to give any Consultant Developed Utility (CDU) a front end to reduce complexity and time required to use them.

As consultants, we constantly see unique situations in the field that cannot be resolved/monitored with Dynatrace out of the box. In many of these situations, we develop our own tools or reports to get the data we need to help the customer get everything into a single pane of glass.

CDUs are generally created for individual customers but, with some tweaking, can be made available to work in any Tenant.

How do I use this portal?

First time? It is recommended to browse the tools/reports to see what the portal offers before getting started


Ok, I browsed, now what?

  1. Use the 'Tenant Configuration' to add any/all tenants you will be working with (can always be found on far right of breadcrumb)
  2. Go to the tool/report you are interested in
  3. Fill in all the required information (Tenants/tags/management-zones will be dropdowns )
  4. Click 'Run'!
  5. You should receive a 'SUCCESS!', 'FAILURE!', or a 'Processing Data...' message (Some tools take time to complete)
  6. DO NOT NAVIGATE AWAY FROM THE PAGE WHILE A TOOL/REPORT IS RUNNING! The page should prevent you from doing this regardless
